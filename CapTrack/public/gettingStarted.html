How to Start your own
Cap Collection

Introduction

So you want to start your own collection of bottle caps? Then you've come to the right place! It's pretty easy to start your own bottle cap collection (and cheap too), just save the cap from your next soda, beer, or even champagne bottle.

Where to find caps

You can get caps not only from your own drinks, but if you keep your eyes out you can often find some interesting ones just laying around.

  • Your local grocery store
  • On city streets
  • Online sellers and auctions
  • Local breweries
  • At a "gathering of like-minded individuals"

Best methods to remove caps

One important aspect of bottle cap collecting is having high quality caps. When removing caps from bottles, some methods leave your cap in better condition than others. Traditional cap removers have the potential to scratch and dent your bottle cap. We have provided some links to products that will help you get your new bottle caps off cleanly.

Finding boards and magnets

You've collected some caps and want a fun way to show them off to your friends and family. A common way to do this is with a board and magnets.
